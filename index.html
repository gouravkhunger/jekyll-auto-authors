This example jekyll site has 2 authors: John Doe, Jane Doe.

John Doe has 6 posts.

Jane Doe has 10 posts.

The pagination setting in _config.yml is set to 5 posts per page. Hence, the author pages show their respective posts and when the number exceeds the per page limit, a pagination trail is shown for navigation.

This is done using the jekyll-auto-authors plugin. Please read the setup article to learn more.
